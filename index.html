BIOGRAPHY

Jenna Ortega is a defining talent of her generation. From her iconic role as Wednesday Addams to her performances in Scream and Beetlejuice, she has become a modern icon of cinema.

Born in California, Jenna has always brought a unique depth to her characters, blending strength with vulnerability.
